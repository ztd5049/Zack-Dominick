About Me

General Information

I'm Zack Dominick, a 20 year old from Pittsburgh PA. All my life I've been more on the artistic side of my family. I've played piano since I was 5 years old, and guitar since I was 18. I love to draw, design, and create works of art through any medium. With photography and videography being two of my favorite passions, I have a youtube channel where I post piano and guitar videos, as well as an instagram where I post pictures I've taken.

Interests and Hobbies

As mentioned above I've played music my whole life. In my spare time I play piano and guitar and record my self doing so to upload to my various social media accounts. Along with that I enjoy hiking and biking, especially in scenic places. I also enjoy playing video games with my friends in my spare time. In addition, I spend time watching youtube videos not only for entertainment, but also to gain knowledge about topics I'm curious about.
