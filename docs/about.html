About Me

General Information

I’m Zack Dominick, a 20-year-old from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Since a very young age, I have shown a strong interest in anything that tapped into my artistic side. I started playing the piano at 5-years-old, dabbled with the ukulele at 10, and dove head-long into playing the guitar at 18. In addition to music, I also enjoy exploring many aspects of the visual arts. I love to draw, design, and create works of art through various mediums. In recent years, these interests have led me to my latest passions, photography and videography. I enjoy sharing my piano and guitar videos on my YouTube channel, as well as my photographs on Instagram.

Interests and Hobbies

As mentioned above, I've played music my whole life. Now that I’m older, I like recording myself playing the piano and guitar and uploading the content to my social media accounts. In addition to music, I enjoy venturing outside to hike and ride my mountain bike on long trails in the Tri-State area. There are so many scenic places to explore and photograph. In my spare time, I enjoy late night gaming sessions with my friends who are now spread across the east coast. Finally, I spend time watching YouTube videos, not only for entertainment, but as a way to gain knowledge and skills related to topics I'm curious about.
